School Excursions

Our tour guides can tailor the content of their presentations to meet the requirements of teachers. In addition to the regular tour activities.

School excursions can include any of the following activities adjusted to suit to the age and curriculum of the students:

  • Scavenger hunt
  • Using orienteering skills to navigate the farm
  • Learning about how our food is produced
  • Making feed for the farm animals
